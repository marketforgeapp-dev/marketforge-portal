\# MASTERFLO PLUMBING — LEAD REPORTING GUIDE  
\#\# How to share booked jobs with MarketForge

\#\#\# Purpose  
MarketForge tracks which campaigns and actions are producing real booked jobs and revenue for Masterflo Plumbing.

To keep this simple, \*\*Masterflo does not need to log into MarketForge to enter leads or jobs\*\*.

Instead, Masterflo only needs to send MarketForge a short update with booked jobs tied to current marketing activity. MarketForge will handle the tracking, attribution, and reporting.

\---

\#\# What Masterflo is responsible for  
Masterflo only needs to provide the following information for each booked job:

1\. Customer name  
2\. Service performed or booked  
3\. Job value / revenue amount  
4\. Date booked  
5\. Any source clue, if known

Examples of source clues:  
\- “Customer mentioned Facebook ad”  
\- “Found us on Google”  
\- “Came from emergency plumbing ad”  
\- “Came from website form”  
\- “Not sure”

If the source is unknown, that is okay. Do not guess.

\---

\#\# What Masterflo does NOT need to do  
Masterflo does \*\*not\*\* need to:  
\- enter data into MarketForge  
\- choose the exact campaign attribution  
\- learn marketing terminology  
\- maintain a complicated spreadsheet  
\- track every phone call that does not become a booked job

MarketForge will handle:  
\- attribution  
\- campaign matching  
\- revenue entry  
\- reporting

\---

\#\# What should be reported  
Report \*\*booked jobs only\*\*.

For now, do not report:  
\- missed calls  
\- unqualified leads  
\- tire-kickers  
\- estimate requests that did not become booked work  
\- duplicate follow-ups for the same customer/job

If one customer books one job, report it once.

\---

\#\# Reporting schedule  
\#\#\# Minimum standard  
Send one update \*\*every Friday\*\*.

\#\#\# Recommended standard  
Send updates \*\*twice per week\*\*:  
\- Tuesday afternoon  
\- Friday afternoon

This keeps reporting current without creating too much burden.

\---

\#\# Preferred reporting format  
Use any of the following:  
\- text message  
\- email  
\- shared note  
\- screenshot from current system  
\- short written list

Use whichever is easiest and fastest.

\---

\#\# Best format to send  
Copy and paste this for each booked job:

Customer Name:  
Service:  
Revenue:  
Date Booked:  
Source Clue (if known):

Example:

Customer Name: John Smith  
Service: Water Heater Replacement  
Revenue: $2,400  
Date Booked: 2026-03-24  
Source Clue: Mentioned Google ad

\---

\#\# If multiple jobs are sent at once  
Send them as a list like this:

\#\#\# Booked Jobs Update

1\.  
Customer Name: Sarah Johnson  
Service: Emergency Plumbing  
Revenue: $850  
Date Booked: 2026-03-24  
Source Clue: Called after seeing Google Business post

2\.  
Customer Name: Michael Davis  
Service: Drain Cleaning  
Revenue: $425  
Date Booked: 2026-03-24  
Source Clue: Not sure

3\.  
Customer Name: Laura Brown  
Service: Water Heater Installation  
Revenue: $2,100  
Date Booked: 2026-03-25  
Source Clue: Mentioned Facebook ad

\---

\#\# If the exact revenue is not known yet  
If the exact revenue is not available, send:  
\- best estimate available now

Then send an update later if the final amount changes.

Example:  
Revenue: Estimated $1,200

\---

\#\# If the service name is unclear  
Use the name your team would naturally use.

Examples:  
\- leak repair  
\- emergency plumbing  
\- drain cleaning  
\- water heater  
\- sewer line  
\- slab leak repair

MarketForge will normalize the service name internally if needed.

\---

\#\# If the source is unclear  
Use one of these:  
\- Not sure  
\- Unknown  
\- Customer did not say

Do not invent a source.

\---

\#\# If one customer books more than one job  
Report each booked job separately if they are clearly separate jobs.

If it is one job with one final invoice, report it once.

\---

\#\# If a job is canceled after being reported  
Send a correction message in this format:

Correction:  
Customer Name:  
Original Date Booked:  
Status Change: Canceled  
Notes:

Example:

Correction:  
Customer Name: Sarah Johnson  
Original Date Booked: 2026-03-24  
Status Change: Canceled  
Notes: Customer postponed indefinitely

\---

\#\# Goal  
The goal is not perfect marketing attribution.  
The goal is to give MarketForge enough visibility to:  
\- track booked jobs  
\- measure revenue impact  
\- determine which actions are working  
\- improve future recommendations

\---

\#\# Summary  
Masterflo’s only job is to send a simple list of booked jobs with:  
\- customer name  
\- service  
\- revenue  
\- date booked  
\- source clue if known

MarketForge handles the rest.
